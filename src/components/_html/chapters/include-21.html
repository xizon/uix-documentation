21. Pagination Styling

If you want to using numeric pagination for your blog and portfolio pages, also allow you change it.

Step 1.
Go to Dashboard » Appearance » Customize.

Step 2.
Locate the "Styling » Paging Navigation" section on the left side of the screen.

Step 3.
You should be able to find option "Numeric Pagination" at this point. If set to "on", the pages module will display numbered pagination instead of Next/Previous links. When you’re done, click "Save & Publish" button.
